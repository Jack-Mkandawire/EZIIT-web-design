1. Sign Up For Free

Individual

Download the Eziit App from Google Playstore or App Store and Sign Up for Free.

The Eziit Individual Account App is the one which is just indicated "Eziit" and the other one which is indicated as "Eziit Merchant" is only for Merchants to be used at point of sale.

Employer

Just go to the sign Up page, fill in the details, select "Employer" on the Select Account Type part and sign Up for free.

Merchant

Just go to the sign Up page, fill in the details, select "Merchant" on the Select Account Type part and sign Up for free.

Financial Instituition

Just go to the sign Up page, fill in the details, select"Financial Institution" on the Select Account Type part and sign Up for free.
