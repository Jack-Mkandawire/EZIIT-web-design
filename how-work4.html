4. Transact

Individual

"Transaction with the individual account" will include:

1. Pont of Sale payments through the Employer linked account,

2. Point of Sale Payments through the Merchant linked acocount,

3. Paying for Others (Sending Vouchers which others can use), and

4. Making Group payments (Contribution for shared expenses)

5. Sending account linking/connecting requests to employer or directly to merchant or Financial Institution,

6. Rating other users to contribute to their Eziit community reputaion collateral

Employer

"Transaction with the Employer account" will include:

1. Approving members of staff requests to link their account with the employer,
<
2. Send connection requests to Merchants and/or financial institutions,

3. Accepting connection requests from Merchants or Financial Institutions

4. Cancelling links for members of staff or connections with merchants and/or financial institutions

5. Rating other users connected with the respective employer and influence their reputation collateral

6. Make payments for goods and services accessed by members of staff during the due period.

Merchant

"Transaction" with the Merchant" account will include:

1. Download for free and be able to use the Eziit Merchant app for point of sale transactions

2. Approving members of staff requests to link their account with the merchant,

3. Send connection requests to employers, individuals and/or financial institutions,

4. Accepting connection requests from employers, individuals and/or Financial Institutions

5. Cancelling links for individuals, connections with employers and/or financial institutions

6. Rating other users connected with the respective merchant and influence their reputation collateral

7. Recieve payments for goods/services accessed by individuals from individuals (for accounts approve by merchant) and employers for accounts linked to employers during the due period.

Financial Instituition

"Transaction with the Financial Institution" account will include:

1. Approving connection requests from employers, merchants or individuals to have their transactions underwritten,

2. Send connection requests to employers, individuals and/or merchants,

3. Accepting connection requests from employers, individuals and/or merchants

5. Cancelling links for individuals, connections with employers and/or merchants

7. Rating other users connected with the respective financial institution and influence their reputation collateral

8. Recieve payments from Eziit (the underwriting cost is embeded in the Transaction cost) for transactions underwritten for individuals from individuals accounts (for accounts approve by merchant) and employers for accounts linked to employers during the due period.
