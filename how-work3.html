3. Approval

Individual

"Approval for individuals" basically refers to Individual Eziit account activation as one can only start using the account with other users who have accepted a connection or linking request from them.

Therefore, this is in a way accepting to conduct business with that individual for merchants or that individual doing business through that employer for employers.

This is a very important stage as it enables employers or merchants to own liability of what you do through your account subject to what has been verified by the respective employer or merchant.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain merchant for accounts not linked to any employer

Employer

"Approval for Employers" basically refers to Employer Eziit account activation as their members of staff can only start using their accounts with merchants who have accepted a connection or request from the employer.

Therefore, a Merchant accepting a request is in a way approving to conduct business with respective employers' members of staff.

This is a very important stage as it enables the employer to express their desire for their members of staff to access goods/services from the respective merchant and the merchant to attest to their willingness to do business with the members of staff of the requesting employer.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain merchants

Merchant

"Approval for Merchants" basically refers to merchant account activation as only members of staff for employers who have accepted a connection or request from the merchant can do business with the respective merchant, this is if the employer had not yet already sent a request to the merchant.

The other aspect could be for a merchant to send a resquest to individual account holders if they are comfortable with the respective individuals ability to settle their account with the merchant on time. Therefore, an employer or individual accepting a request is in a way approving to conduct business with the respective merchant.

This is a very important stage as it enables the merchant to express their desire to allow members of staff for a respective employer or an individual to access their goods/services and the employer or indidvidual to attest to their willingness to do business with the merchant and commitment to settle their accounts within the default 30 days or any agreed upon period.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain employers or individuals

Financial Instituition

"Approval for financial institutions" basically refers to Financial Institution Eziit account activation by employers, individual account holders or merchants as underwrting eziit transactions is optional for all users subject to the requirements of those they would want to do business with.

Therefore, an employer, individual account holder or Merchant accepting a request is in a way approving to have the respective financial institution underwrite their transactions.

This is a very important stage as it enables the financial institution to express their desire to underwrite tranasctions for respective employers, individual account holders or merchants and these partys to attest to their willingness to have the aforementioned done for them by the financial institution.
