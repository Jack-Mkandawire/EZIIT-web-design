2. Link Account

Individual

"Linking Individual Account" refers to Connecting your Individual Eziit acount to your Employer, Merchant or bothusing their Eziit Employer or Merchant account holder identification codes which are given to all Eziit account holders after opening an account and can be seen in their respective accounts after loging in.

This is the first step in activating your Eziit account and enables employers or merchants to verify your details and allocate an amount they will allow you to spend through eziit based on your net income for the employer or reputation for merchants.

Employer

"Linking Employer Account" refers to connecting your account as an Employer with merchants or Financial Institutions (optional)using their Eziit Codes. This is basically seeking permision from a merchant for your members of staff to access their goods/services.

It also servis as a commitment that you will deduct through your payroll and settle all outstanding payments within 30days or any agreed upon period. In the case of financial institutions, if needed in order for your staff to transact with certain merchants, it is basically asking if they could underwrite transactions for your members of staff.

Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Employer Eziit account and enables merchants and Financial institutions (if needed) to verify your details and agree to allow your staff access their goods/services for merchnats or underwrite transactions for financial institutions.

Merchant

"Linking Merchant Account" refers to connecting your Eziit Merchant account to Employers, individual Eziit acoount holders or financial institutions using their Eziit Codes found in all eziit account holders accounts. This is basically offering your goods/services to members of staff of a respective employer to be paid for through the payroll wihin 30 days or an agreed upon period, or individuals with a good reputaion through past transactions with you or other trusted merchants.

Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Merchant Eziit account if no requests have been sent to you by other Eziit users and enables employers or individuals to verify your details and agree to do business with you. In the case of financial institutions, it is asking them if the could underwrite transactions with respective emlpoyers or individuals acccessing your goods/ services through eziit.

Financial Instituition

"Linking Financial Institution Account" refers to connecting your Eziit Financial Institution account to Employers, individuals with Eziit acoounts or Merchants using their Eziit Codes. This is basically offering your services as an underwriter to Employers, individuals or Merchants.

Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Financial Institution Eziit account if no requests have been sent to you by other Eziit users and enables employers, Merchants or individuals to verify your details and agree to do business with you.
