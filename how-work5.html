5. Payments

Individual

"Payments with the individual account" will include:

1. Payment through employer payroll for Employer linked accounts ,

2. Direct payments to Merchants for Merchant approved accounts,

Other Payments

1. Transanction fee of ZMK10.00 or USD $1.00 (International Users),

2. Applicable fees for using other platforms like mobile money, Visa, Internet Banking fees, Bank money tranfer fees, etc for Merchant approved accounts,

2. Applicable fees for currency conversion for International Transactions,

Note: All other payments shall appear in real time on the statement in the Users account.

Employer

Payments with the Employer account will include:

1. Making payroll based payments to Merchants for all due transactions for Members of staff,

2. Making Payroll based payments to Eziit or Financial Institutions who underwrote your members of staff transactions,

Other Payments

1. Transanction fee of ZMK00.00 or USD $0.00 (International Users),

2. Applicable fees for using other platforms like mobile money, Visa, Internet Banking Fess, Bank Transfer fees, etc for Merchant approved accounts,

3. Applicable fees for currency conversion for International Transactions,

Note: All other payments shall appear in real time on the statement in the Users account.

Merchant

"Payments with the Merchant" account will include:

1. Receiving payments from Employers for all due amounts for their members of staff,

2. Receiving payments from Individuals for those with accounts approved by the respective Merchant,

3. Making payments (optional) to Eziit or Financial Institutions who underwrote transactions if Merchant requested for the Underwriting service,

Other Payments

1. Transanction fee of ZMK00.00 or USD $0.00 (International Users),

2. Applicable fees for using other platforms like mobile money, Visa, Internet Banking Fess, Bank Transfer fees, etc for Merchant approved accounts,

3. Applicable fees for currency conversion for International Transactions,
Note: All other applicable payment cost (if applicable) shall appear in real time on the statement in the Users account.

Financial Instituition

"Payments with the Financial Institution" account will include:

1. Receiving payments from Employers or Eziit for all transactions underwriten for respective Employer linked accounts,

2. Receiving payments from Merchants for those with accounts approved by the respective Merchant,

Other Payments

1. Transanction fee of ZMK00.00 or USD $0.00 (International Users),

2. Applicable fees for using other platforms like mobile money, Visa, Internet Banking Fess, Bank Transfer fees, etc for Merchant approved accounts,

3. Applicable fees for currency conversion for International Transactions,

Note: All other applicable payment cost (if applicable) shall appear in real time on the statement in the Users account.
